DaemonSet ensures that one copy of the POD is always present in all members of a cluster

Use Case
	Monitoring Solution
	Logs Viewer
	
	
pod-definition.yml
apiVersion: v1
kind: DaemonSet
metedata:
	name: monitoring-daemon
spec:
	selector:
		matchLabels:
			app: monitoring-agent
	template:
		metadata:
			labels:
				app: monitoring-agent
		spec:
			containers:
				- name: monitoring-agent
				image: monitoring-agent
				
kubectl create -f daemon-set-definiton.yml
